Welcome to my journal for Hypersomnia Project, an upcoming multiplayer top-down shooter.

Here you shall witness how a dream project comes true, day after day, one little step at a time.

I'll do my best to publish new posts at least once in a week.
I will document new features or just write down my thoughts about the game.

Some technicalities:
- This website is hosted via Github Pages.
- It is powered by Jekyll.
- It uses an own-rolled external comments system.

If you want to contribute to this blog, you can always fork the website's official repository.
This way you can even suggest a new post!

Subscribe via RSS to be kept up to date with latest changes.
Alternatively, let me know your e-mail so that you won't miss any post.
