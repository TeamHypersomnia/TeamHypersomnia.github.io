If this does not work for some reason, follow these steps in your TeamSpeak3 client:

    2. Choose Connect.
    3. Paste ts3.hypersomnia.pl in the Server Address field.
    4. Fill in your Nickname, leave Server Password field empty and click Connect.
    5. Pick any of the channels inside PARLEMENT OF HYPERSOMNIA section, we will be there.
